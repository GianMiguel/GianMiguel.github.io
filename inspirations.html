Culture
Culture opens the sense of beauty.
The Philippines is a culture in which East meets West. The Filipino people have a distinct Asian background,
with a strong Western tradition. The modern Filipino culture developed through influence
from Chinease traders, Spanish conquistadors, and American rulers.

  • Calle Crisologo
  • National Museum

  • Panagbenga Festival

Nature
Look deep into nature, and then
you will understand everything better.
Because of its archipelagic nature, Philippines is a culturally diverse country. With its topography
consisting of mountainous terrains, dense forests, plains, and coastal areas,
the Philippines is rich in biodiversity.
  • Mayon Volcano
  • Banaue Rice Terraces
  • Mines View Park
  • Tagaytay
Food
Food to the Filipino is History. It is also a bond, culture, and identity.
Because of the country's colonial history, Filipino food has evolved into a melting pot of eastern and western influences,
so much so that some writers have described it as “Asian fusion before Asian fusion even existed”.
