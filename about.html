About us
Introducing
Tara Ride Magazine
Tara ride is an E-Magazine created for the convenience of Motorcycle Enthusiast.

For riders, riding out regardless of the destination is an adventure itself. An E-Magazine that features places of riders' favorite destinations, routes, shops/restaurants, and activities. There is a lot of reason why there is a slogan that says "It is more fun in the Philippines". There are 7,461 islands that translates to numerous location that a rider can choose to go here in the Philippines.

As Luzon is the archipelago's largest island. You can actually travel from Manila to another province by land just riding your motorcycle. This E-Magazine will be presenting beautiful places from Luzon to you.
